Homemade Chili

Description

This homemade chili is loaded with beef, beans, and crowd-pleasing flavor.

It also may contain a certain degree of human body parts sprinkled in there just for laughs. Don't judge me.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Canned Goods
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook the beef and the onion.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients, then bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat and let simmer for 15 min.
